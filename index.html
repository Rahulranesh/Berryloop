Memora

A vintage-themed digital scrapbook app built entirely in Flutter. Drag, resize, and rotate Polaroid photos, handwritten sticky notes, and analog stickers on a virtual canvas—no cloud required.

Our Design Process

How we approach building every Berry Loop application — from concept to release.

Identify the Gap

We look for common pain points where existing solutions are overbuilt, cloud-dependent, or riddled with ads and trackers. If we can solve it locally — we build it.

01
02

Architect Offline-First

Every feature is designed to function without internet. We use local databases, on-device ML, and hardware-accelerated encoding to ensure zero dependency on remote servers.

Design with Discipline

We apply geometric design principles — clean typography, purposeful whitespace, and spring-loaded micro-interactions. Every pixel serves a function.

03
04

Ship & Iterate

We release lean, focused builds on the Play Store. Then iterate based on crash-free metrics, performance profiling, and real user feedback — not data harvesting.

Offline-first.
No trackers. No noise.

We believe that utilities should be utility-focused, not data farms. All computing runs locally on your hardware. No accounts, no subscriptions, and no internet required. Your tools load instantly, run infinitely, and keep your data where it belongs: with you.

Zero Servers

Every computation happens on your local device. No remote servers, no cloud dependency.

Privacy First

No accounts, no trackers, no analytics pixels. Your data stays encrypted and local.

Local Computing

Hardware-accelerated processing using your device's full capabilities, offline and instant.
